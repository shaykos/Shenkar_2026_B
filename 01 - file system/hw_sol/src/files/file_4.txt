Here's a version featuring Snow White:

Once upon a time, in a kingdom surrounded by dark forests, there lived a princess named Snow White. Known for her beauty and kindness, she had skin as white as snow, lips as red as blood, and hair as black as ebony.

Her stepmother, the vain Queen, could not bear Snow White's beauty and grew jealous. One day, she ordered a huntsman to take Snow White into the forest and end her life. But the huntsman, moved by her innocence, let her escape.

Snow White fled through the forest until she discovered a small cottage. Inside, she found seven tiny chairs, seven tiny plates, and seven tiny beds. She cleaned the cottage and made a simple meal, then fell asleep.

When the seven dwarfs returned home, they found their cottage tidied and discovered Snow White. They took pity on her and allowed her to stay, and she became like a sister to them.

The Queen eventually discovered Snow White was alive and, disguised as an old woman, gave her a poisoned apple. Snow White took a single bite and fell into an enchanted sleep. It was only when a passing prince kissed her that she awoke, and they lived happily ever after.
